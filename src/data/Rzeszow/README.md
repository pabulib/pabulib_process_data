Rzeszow data notes
==================

2026
----

The city provided project results and anonymized ballot data in response to
public information request KP-P.3027.3.2026.ŁCH.

The citywide pool covers city infrastructure projects. District projects are
split into neighborhood files because the official rule applies a separate
neighborhood budget cap: 300,000 PLN for neighborhoods with up to 2,000
registered residents and 400,000 PLN for neighborhoods above 2,000 registered
residents. The Social Activities Budget covers pro-social, cultural,
educational, or sports activities.

The city project/results sheet omits project 36 from Generała Grota Roweckiego,
but the public voting list includes it and valid ballots contain 94 indications
for it. We add project 36 from the voting list and count its votes from the
ballots.
